Cebu Dataset
------------------------------------------
Raster:

*altitude - from worldclim
*asci layers - final data for maxent
*slope - derived from 1km alt
*vegetation - from alos palsar 2010
*worldclim - bio1-19 data from worldclim

Vector:

*black shama occurrence records - from FFI survey
*iba
*municipal boundary
*river 
*road
*soil - from dswm

Projection: geographic, WGS84 datum
