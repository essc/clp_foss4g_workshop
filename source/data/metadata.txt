Calayan dataset
--------------------------------------------

Species occurence samples
 * Gallirallus_calayanensis_raw.xls -   Calayan Rail (Gallirallus calayanensis) 2006 inventory by Isla Biodiversity.

Environmental variables (Raster)
 * bio1_210 - Annual mean temperature
 * bio12_210 - Annual precipitation
 * bio19_210 - Precipitation of Coldest Quarter
 * elevation - Digital elevation model
 * slope - gradient of a line describing steepness, incline, or grade of the surface derived from elevation

Vector
 * calayancoast - Calayan island coastline from Cynthia Layusa.
 * admin_bnd - Calayan barangay boundaries from PhilGis.org
 
Projection - Geographic projection, WGS84 datum.
